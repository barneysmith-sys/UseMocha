Last updated: April 2025  ·  Mocha is built and operated by Barney Smith

Mocha is an AI-powered behavioral interview simulator, currently in early beta. These terms explain how it works, what data we collect, and what you can expect. We've kept the language as simple as possible — this is a student-built product and we want to be straightforward with you.

1. What Mocha Is

Mocha is a behavioral interview coaching tool. You answer practice questions, and an AI evaluates your answer using frameworks like the McKinsey PEI rubric, Amazon Leadership Principles, and similar industry standards. You get a score, structured feedback, and a suggested stronger answer.

Mocha is designed for college students and early-career professionals preparing for interviews at consulting firms, banks, tech companies, and similar employers. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any of the companies or firms referenced in the feedback.

2. Beta Disclaimer

Mocha is in beta. This means it's a work in progress. Things may break, change, or disappear. The AI feedback is generated by Google's Gemini model and is meant to be useful coaching guidance — not a guarantee of interview success or a formal assessment of your abilities.

We do our best to make the feedback accurate and fair, but it's AI-generated and you should use your own judgment alongside it. Don't treat a Mocha score as an official evaluation.

3. Using Mocha

By using Mocha, you agree to:

  • Use it for your own interview preparation — not to misrepresent your abilities to employers
  • Not attempt to abuse, overload, or manipulate the AI or the platform
  • Not share your account with others
  • Be at least 16 years old (or have parental consent if younger)

Mocha is free to use. We don't charge you anything and we don't process any payments.

4. Data We Collect

We collect very little. Here's exactly what:

  • Your name and email — collected when you create an account, via Formspree. This is used to identify your account and occasionally send product updates.
  • Your interview answers — sent to Google's Gemini API to generate feedback. We don't store your answers on our servers after the feedback is returned.
  • Usage data — we use Google Analytics to understand how people use Mocha (which pages are visited, how long sessions last, etc.). This data is anonymised and aggregated.
  • Local storage — your practice history, streak, and settings are stored in your browser's local storage. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.

5. How We Use Your Data

Your name and email are used to:

  • Create and identify your account
  • Occasionally send you product updates or improvements (you can ask us to stop at any time)

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not share your personal information with advertisers. We do not build profiles for marketing purposes.

6. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use Mocha — things like which features are used most, how long sessions last, and where users drop off. This helps us improve the product.

Google Analytics collects anonymised data about your browser, device, and behaviour on the site. It does not collect your name or email. You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

7. Third-Party Services

Mocha uses the following third-party services to function:

  • Vercel — hosting and serverless functions. Your requests pass through Vercel's infrastructure.
  • Google Gemini API — the AI that generates your interview feedback. Your answers are sent to Google's servers to generate responses. Google's own privacy policy applies to this processing.
  • Formspree — handles signup form submissions. Your name and email are stored in Formspree's dashboard.
  • Google Analytics — usage tracking, as described above.

8. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to keep your data safe. API keys are stored securely on Vercel's servers and never exposed in frontend code. Your password is stored locally in your browser only — we don't have access to it.

That said, no system is perfectly secure. If you have any concerns about your data, contact us and we'll address them promptly.

9. Your Rights

You can:

  • Ask us to delete your name and email from our records at any time
  • Ask what data we hold about you
  • Opt out of product update emails
  • Clear your local practice history at any time by clearing your browser's local storage

To do any of the above, email us at the address below.

10. Changes and Availability

Because Mocha is in beta, things will change. We may update features, pause the service, or shut it down entirely. We'll try to give notice if anything significant changes, but we can't guarantee uninterrupted access.

We may update these Terms and Privacy Policy as the product evolves. If we make significant changes, we'll update the date at the top of this page. Continued use of Mocha after changes means you accept the updated terms.

11. Limitation of Liability

Mocha is provided as-is, with no guarantees. We're not responsible for interview outcomes, hiring decisions, or any consequences of using the feedback provided. The AI feedback is coaching guidance only.
