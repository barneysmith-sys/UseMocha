What Mocha is

Mocha is an AI behavioral interview coach. You answer practice questions and get scored feedback against real-world evaluation frameworks used by top firms in consulting, finance, and tech.

Mocha is not affiliated with or endorsed by any firm or company referenced in the feedback. Those names describe publicly known hiring standards only.

Beta disclaimer

Mocha is a work in progress. Things may break or change. Feedback is AI-generated coaching guidance, not a formal assessment. Don't treat a Mocha score as an official evaluation.

Liability

Mocha is provided as-is. We're not responsible for interview outcomes or hiring decisions. Use the feedback alongside your own judgment.

Using Mocha

By using Mocha you agree to:

Data we collect

We collect as little as possible.

  • Name & email at signup, to identify your account and send occasional product updates
  • Interview answers processed in real time to generate your feedback, not stored afterwards
  • Usage analytics anonymised data via Google Analytics — no name, no email, just behaviour patterns
  • Local data your history, streak, and settings live in your browser only and never leave your device
How we use your data

Your name and email create your account and let us send product updates. You can opt out any time.

We never sell your data. No advertisers. No profiling.

Your rights

Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use Mocha. It doesn't collect your name or email. Opt out anytime via the GA opt-out add-on.

Security

All credentials are stored server-side and never exposed in frontend code. Your password lives in your browser only. We'll notify you promptly if anything goes wrong.
