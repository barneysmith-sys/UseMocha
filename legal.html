Mocha is an AI-powered behavioral interview simulator, currently in early beta. These terms explain how it works, what data we collect, and what you can expect. We've kept the language as simple as possible this is a student-built product and we want to be straightforward with you.

1. What Mocha Is

Mocha is a behavioral interview coaching tool. You answer practice questions and an AI evaluates your answer using frameworks like the McKinsey PEI rubric, Amazon Leadership Principles, and similar industry standards. You get a score, structured feedback, and a suggested stronger answer.

Mocha is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to any of the companies or firms referenced in the feedback. Those names are used only to describe publicly known evaluation standards.

2. Beta Disclaimer

Mocha is in beta a work in progress. Things may break, change, or disappear. The AI feedback is generated by Google's Gemini model and is meant as coaching guidance only, not a guarantee of interview success or a formal assessment of your abilities. Don't treat a Mocha score as an official evaluation.

3. Using Mocha

By using Mocha, you agree to:

  • Use it for your own interview preparation only
  • Not attempt to abuse, overload, or manipulate the AI or the platform
  • Not share your account with others
  • Be at least 16 years old

Mocha is free. We don't charge you anything and we don't process any payments.

4. Data We Collect

We collect very little. Here's exactly what:

  • Your name and email collected when you sign up via Formspree, used to identify your account and occasionally send product updates.
  • Your interview answers sent to Google's Gemini API to generate feedback. Your answers are not stored on our servers after the response is returned.
  • Usage data Google Analytics collects anonymised data about how people use Mocha. It does not collect your name or email.
  • Local storage your practice history, streak, and settings are saved in your browser only and never leaves your device.

5. How We Use Your Data

Your name and email are used to create your account and occasionally send product updates. You can ask us to stop at any time.

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not share your information with advertisers. We do not build profiles for marketing purposes.

6. Google Analytics

We use Google Analytics to understand how people use Mocha which features are used most, how long sessions last, and where users drop off. This helps us improve the product. You can opt out at any time using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

7. Third-Party Services

Mocha relies on:

  • Vercel hosting. Your requests pass through their infrastructure.
  • Google Gemini API the AI that generates your feedback. Your answers are sent to Google's servers. Google's privacy policy applies.
  • Formspree handles signups. Your name and email are stored in their dashboard.
  • Google Analytics anonymised usage tracking as described above.

8. Security

API keys are stored securely on Vercel's servers and never exposed in frontend code. Your password is stored locally in your browser only we don't have access to it. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable precautions and will let you know promptly if anything affecting your data goes wrong.

9. Your Rights

You can ask us to:

  • Delete your name and email from our records
  • Tell you what data we hold about you
  • Stop sending you product updates

Just email us at the address below and we'll take care of it.

10. Liability

Mocha is provided as-is. We're not responsible for interview outcomes, hiring decisions, or any consequences of using the feedback. The AI coaching is guidance only use your own judgment alongside it.

11. Changes & Availability

We may update these terms, change features, or shut the service down this is beta. We'll update the date at the top of this page when anything important changes. Continued use means you accept the updated terms.
